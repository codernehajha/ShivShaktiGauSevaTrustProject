Shiv Shakti Gou Seva Trust

Gau Seva Trust is dedicated to the welfare of cows and promoting traditional agricultural practices. Our mission is to protect and nurture cows, spread awareness about their importance, and encourage ethical dairy farming. We conduct rescue operations, provide medical aid, and run awareness programs to educate people about the significance of Gau Seva.

More About Shiv Shakti Gau Seva Trust

Founded in 2024

Shiv Shakti Gau Seva Trust was established in Ambedkar Nagar with a mission to protect, shelter, and care for abandoned and injured cows. The trust was founded by Shri Chandresh Yadav with the vision of promoting cow welfare and sustainable cow-based farming.
