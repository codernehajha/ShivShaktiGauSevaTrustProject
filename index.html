The Sacred Duty of Gau Seva

Cows hold a sacred place in our culture, symbolizing purity, nourishment, and selfless service. Gau Seva is not just an act of kindness but a divine responsibility to care for these gentle beings who provide us with nourishment and sustenance. Protecting and nurturing cows ensures the preservation of nature‚Äôs balance, supporting a harmonious coexistence between humans and animals. By offering food, shelter, and medical care, we uphold our ethical and spiritual duty, contributing to a world where compassion and respect for all living beings flourish.

Welcome to Shiv Shakti Gau Seva Trust

Founded in **[2024]**, **Shiv Shakti Gau Seva Trust, Ambedkar Nagar**, is dedicated to the protection and well-being of cows. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and provide shelter to abandoned and injured cows while promoting organic farming and sustainable living.
